7 Mond Light S. Westerly and cloudy
 at 9 am I came on board the Mejidieh, she is alongside the Blosse Lynch shipping cargo out of her
 The Redbreast left this morning for down the river to patrol the river between this & Fao
 The S.S. Phrat arrived last night at 10 3/4 P.M.
 At 12 am I took Cowley's son Douglas with me to Rufails house where he stopped for 2 or 3 hours & sent him back
 Jeboory Asfar called on us in the evening, he says that the Arabistan has left Marseilles on the 31st Octor
 ~ Weather became fine with a Light n.w. breeze
