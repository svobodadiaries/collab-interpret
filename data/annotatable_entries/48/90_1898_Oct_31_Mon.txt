31 Mond therm @ 6.60 Light N.W. & fine weather at 8 am I came on board and paid the ship’s company their wages for this month, I went to the office to Mr. Bottomley for some questions regarding the pay of some people
 Came home at 12
 I took a warm bath
 At 4 P.M. called on Eliza [illegible] also on Sister Medula & came home at 5 ¼ Johny & Artin called on me till 7 P.M.
