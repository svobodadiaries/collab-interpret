3 Frid Therm. @ 6.44 1899 Feby Light N.W. & fine weather At 6,,15 Am passed Memlah
 I slept from 1 am till about 5, but I feel weak & not refreshing, At 6,,45 we passed the S.S. Baghdad & Barge bound up
 There is a little rise in the river~ Gave tickets to 11 Coot passengers,~ The SS. Mossul was at Coot last night , bound down she was on the opposite side along Eassayi's Press, shipping  Liquorice
 At 2,,30 P.M. passed Azizieh Village
 At 5,,50 P.M. passed the S.S. Khalifah going down just at Baghdadieh Fort ~ Light N.W. and fine weather
 I turned in at 8 1/2 but again I could not get a sleep, The palpitation of my heart & fright & the ringing of the Bell, although I drunk Punch & am very sleepy
 at 12 I took my bed out on the hatch & laid down it is cold, but I have lost my sleep & could not dose off
