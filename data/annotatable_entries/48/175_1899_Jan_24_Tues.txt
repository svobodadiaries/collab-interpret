24 Tuesd Therm 6.34 1899 Jany Light n.w. & cold weather
 at 8 am I called on Menusha, Medoola my sister, Antone Marine & sister Eliza & came home at 11 am ~ The S.S. Baghdad left this morning at 4 1/2 am sister Medula called on us
 I took a warm bath I feel still very nervous & weak, I am sleeping well, but have much debility~
