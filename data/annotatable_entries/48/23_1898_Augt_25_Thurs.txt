25 Thursd Light N.W. & calm warm weather
 At 7 am Rufail & I went in to Basreh to Tookyeh where we were forced by them to Stop & breakfast with them
 At 0 1/2 P.M. We left and came out to the house, It is very hot, but there is Wind though it is not pleasant, but warm & moisty~ the night was cool with a Modte N.W. Wind The S.S. Mossul passed down to Basreh at midnight
