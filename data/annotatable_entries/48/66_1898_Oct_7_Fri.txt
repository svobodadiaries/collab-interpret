 At 12 passed  Memlah
 At 2,,10 P.M. anchored to Sound at Um el Ajaj~ At 2,,45 proceeded, ~ At 5,,30 P.M. arrived at Coot, landed 20 passengers & 14 packages
 the S.S. Mossul & Barge are here having left Baghdad a day before us ~ Very light air & clouds on the horyzon ~ We remained at Coot for the night
