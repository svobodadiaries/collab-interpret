12th Wedne The @ 6.34, Noon 43 Light N.W. & foggy, the fog is passing on & the sun come out for few minutes & it again became thick, At 8 ½ AM I came on board
 the Mejidieh is inside discharging
 At 11 I went home, weather Keeps very cloudy & overcast no sun & is very cold, I called on Yacoob Pahlawan’s family & at 2 P.M. Came on board
 We are shipping & discharging cargo
 At 4 ½ P.M went home it is raining drizzly for ½ hour
 At 7 it cleared off & became fine~
