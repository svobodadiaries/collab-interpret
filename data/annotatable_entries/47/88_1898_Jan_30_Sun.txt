30 Sund Th @ 6.33 Light n.w. & fine cold morning At 6.30 am we proceeded At 8 passed the SS. Mossul & Barge going up~ At 9,,20 passed Ali Gherbi
 At 5,,45 P.M. arrived at Amara landed 20 1/2 Passeng. and 53 Packages
 They have had lots of rain here a few days ago until yesterday
 We took 4 Tons of Coal, At 7,,30 we left Amara, fine clear weather & moon light night 8 days old~ At 11 we anchored above Kalat Saleh~
