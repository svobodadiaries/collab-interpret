4 Satur The @ 6. 55 Very light N.W. air & thick fog dampy weather~ It had rained at 3 Am for a short time~ At 10 fog cleared off We got underway~ At 1,,40 P.M. passed Memlah
 lots of rain has fallen here about
 At 3,,45 grounded at Umel Ajaj, took an anchor ahead & hove off~ at 4,,30 proceeded~ Fine Clear Weather~ At 7,,15 P.M. arrived at Coot landed 20 passengers and 86 Packages
 I received a packet of news paper from Alexander via Bombay dated the 24 Octor
 we remained at anchor for the night
 Fine clear weather~
