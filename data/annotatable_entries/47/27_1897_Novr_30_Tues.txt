30" Tuesd Light S. Westerly & cloudy gloomy weather
 at 8 am I came on board Paid the Ship's Company their wages for this month
 The SS. Ressafah left this Morning at 6 am~ At 11 I left the ship & went home~ Called on Catterina Sayegh & her sons in her father's house (or brother Yousif) to condole her on her brother Yousif's death she is receiving the Visitors there for the three days & came home at 5
