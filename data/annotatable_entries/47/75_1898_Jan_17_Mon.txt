17 Mond The @ 6.39 Light N.W. & fine clear Weather & sunshine~ At 9,,20 landed Eleven packages at Ali Gherbi and went on at 9.35~ At 5,,35 P.M. arrived at Amara landed 24 passengers and 31 packages
 also 14 men of Lynch's (Mr. Tom Tartt, Jejo Moorad, Rezooki Georgis & 10 men to try the Wool Press & return with us~ At 7,,20 we left Amara, fine clear weather, and very cold~ At 10,,45 anchored above Kalat Saleh~
