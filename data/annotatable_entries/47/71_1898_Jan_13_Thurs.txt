13th Thursd The @ 6.32 Light N.W. & fine clear weather, the first day we have seen the sun & very cold~ At 8 ½ AM I came on board, The S.S. Baghdad left at about 7 ½ AM I finished Alexander's letter for today's post & enclosed for him copies of N. Sayegh's letter & mine to him~ I also wrote to Miss Tanner from my wifes part, & N. Sayegh's letter~ At 11½ went to breakfast & returned at 1 P.M
 I sent the letters to the Post Office & got the one for Alexander registered, We finished at 4½ P.M. I went home~ Weather getting cloudy a little~
