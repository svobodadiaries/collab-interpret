9 Thursd 1897 Decr Light S. Erly & cloudy all over gloomy weather~ At 7,,55 landed 2 passengers at Ali Gherbi & took 7 and went on at 8~ Gave tickets to 19 1/2 Amara passengers, and Isak Ezra Hyim in 1st Class Retn Ticket by S Lynch co free~ at about 3 P.M. the breeze shifted to N.W. & Clouds breaking up~ At Sunset it became very fine & cold N.W. breeze
 At 7,,45 P.m. Arrived at Coot landed 15 passengers and took in Coal 7 Tons, The S.S. Phrat is here on her way down~ At 9,,20 left Coot~
