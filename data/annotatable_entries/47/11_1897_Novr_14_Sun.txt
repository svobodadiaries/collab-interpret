14 Sund The @ 6.33 N.W. breeze & very cold weather
 it is so sudden & so bitterly cold, the water frose in some houses~ At 9 am I went to church & then called on Chekury Andrea, Yacoob Oossy, Eassayis, Kass Phillippus etc
 many visitors came to visit Eliza my wife~ I called on Sister Eliza, Henry called on us, It is very cold throughout the day
~ This is very unusual cold which came on so soon & sudden
 we are not prepared for it yet,
