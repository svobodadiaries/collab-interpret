17 Tuesd Therm @ 5,,70 1898 May Light or calm weather At 4 1/2 am I came on board we are discharging cargo, I went home at 11
 Weather got very warm & calm with light S. Ely breeze
. My sisters Eliza & Emilia also Alice & Terrooza called on us this afternoon
 Weather kept very warm through the night
