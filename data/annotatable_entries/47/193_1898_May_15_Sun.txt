15 Sund 1898 May Light Ely breeze & calm weather ~ at 8 am Eliza & I went to church, I then called on the Assyrian Bishop on Polus Serkis, Shekoory Andrea, Sister Medula Menusha etc Antone Marine Hannosh Asfar & Habib Chiha called on us also Johnny & Arlin & Chekoory Sayegh
 At 2 P.M. I called on Antone Marine & then we went & called on Sister Eliza & on Alice & Emilia, I left Anton at Alice & went to call on Dr. Rozen the German Consul but he was not at home, I left my card, & came back to Alice & we left at 6 ~
