27 Wedn. 1898 April At 4 AM. proceeded~ Light S. Erly breeze & clouds disappearing
 I took a dose of Castor Oil this morning early at 4 ½ as I have felt billious and bowels out of order, I had not taken it for a year, We took 24 ½ passengers from Coot~ River is falling much, At 9 Am passed Memlah, Wind shifted to a fresh N.W. but it is hazy~ At 5,,50 P.M. passed Azizieh fine Clear Weather~ At 9,,40 passed Baghdadieh Fort~ Light N.W. & fine weather, Cool night
