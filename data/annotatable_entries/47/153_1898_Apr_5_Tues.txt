5th Tuesd. Light N.W. & hazy with some clouds~ At 8 am came on board, we are discharging, and also shipping cargo
 The Jew will go into their holyday on Thursday~ At 10 went to call on Sister Medula & then came home had breakfast at 1 P.M. I came on board~ We shipped some through cargo, there is Vartan taking and Mr. Reynolds the Chief Mate is out Cricketing
 I left at 5 Came home, Weather Cloudy Antone Marine called, also Eliahoo Denoos the Residency Banker~
