31' Tuesd 1898 June Light N.W. & fine
 Today is the 10th of Moharrem being the mourning day for the Hassan & Hussein among the Shia sect~ at 7 1/2 A.M. I came on board~ The Blosse is alongside discharging cargo~ The Mossul is also loading a great deal of cargo & Bales of wool~ at 4 1/2 I went home~ Light N.W. & fine Weather,
