11 Satur Therm @ 5, 74 Modte N.W. & fine cool Morning, at 7 Am I came on board, the Blosse is alongside the wharf
 discharging cargo, At 10 went home~ I took a warm bath
 The weather is hot during the day a warm breeze blowing 
 The nights are very cool~ The wife & children of Jeboory Asfar have hired the house of Joojoo the Goldsmith close to my house where Mr. Nelson & DeMello were living, at 50 [illegible] per year~
