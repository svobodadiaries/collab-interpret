26th Satur The @ 5.47 Modte N.W. Wind & very fine weather~ At 5.30 AM. We proceeded from below Sheikh Saad
 At 8.20 passed Ali Gherbi, At 3,,50 P.M. arrived at Amara landed 12 passengers (H. Abdulghani Khdery in 1st Class free) and 4 packages, Here too it rained yesterday for about 4 hours~ At 4,,35 left Amara, Took 9 passengers
 At 6,,20 we rounded and Came to below Aboo Sedra, fine clear weather, still day- light & moonlight
 a pity we did not go as far as Kalat Saleh~
