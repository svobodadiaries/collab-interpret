6th Wedn. Light N.W. & cloudy all over sultry~ At 8 am I came on board~ Finished Alexander's letter for tomorrow's Post, also a letter for the Handels Museums in Vienna
 We finished loading at 4 P.M. having shipped a great deal of cargo Mostly gallnuts, I went home, at 5, Eliza was at Church I also went for a short time there were Prayers & Service for the Good Friday
~ Artin called on us~ Weather cloudy,
