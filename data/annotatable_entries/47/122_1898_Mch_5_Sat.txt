5th Satur The @ 5.47 Light n.w. & fine weather
 at 8 1/2 am I came on board the SS. Mossul is alongside discharging & loading
 At 10 I called on Menusha, I took a warm bath
~ Lots of theft & plunder are taking place in town & outside, the Arabs are plundering Keleks & caravans
 & theives robbing houses & shops on account of the scarcity~
