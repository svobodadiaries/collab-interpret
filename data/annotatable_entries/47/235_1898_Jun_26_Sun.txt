26' Sund Therm@ 5,72 Light N.W. & fine~ at 7 1/2 A.M. I called on Antone Marine & then we went to church, I then called on Nessoory Andrea who has come up from Basreh for 15 days leave & is going down with us
 Called on the Assyrian Bishop, on the Chaldean Patriarch Abed Ysho, Catterina Yaghchi, Sister Medula, Menusha & came home at 12
 at 2 1/2 P.M. called on Eassayee brothers
 Shemoony & her sons of Korkis, on Sister Eliza & Alice, came home at 6~ Light air and not so cool but pleasant,
