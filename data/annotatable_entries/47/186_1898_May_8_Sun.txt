8 Sund S. Erly wind cloudy Sultry & warm~ The mail Steamer Assyria left early this morning At 7 Am I came down to the Blosse Lynch, we are shipping cargo, & have to leave tomorrow~ At 11 Rezooki & I went in to Basreh called on Tookyeh & her husband Rezooki & at 2 P.M. came out to the steamer
~ At 4 we then went up to Rufails house, and at 6 we all came to Jeboory Asfar where we dined & returned at 10 P.M.
 A fine Modte N.W. wind is blowing, but there are a lot of sandflies & some mosquitoes~
