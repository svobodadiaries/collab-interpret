28 Wedn. Therm @ 4,76 [A49_223_18:154] Light n.w. & fine cool weather ~ at 7 am my wife & I called on Antone Marine & then on Mina Abood, came home at 11 am. ~ I took a cold bath, Eliza & Adoola my cousins called on us ~ I went to Sister Medula at 5 ~ at 6 1/2 Eliza & I called on the Yaghchies on top of their house & spent the evening till 9 1/2 & came home, The S.S. Ressafah & Barge arrived at 7 1/2 P.m.
 Light n.w. & fine ~
