18 Tuesd, Therm @ 4.80, Noon 104, 4 P.M. 111 July Light Erly breeze and Sultry weather
 Clouds on the Est & South~ At 4,,30 AM proceeded from Seyd Abbass
~ At 6,,15 passed Ali Gherbi
 ~At 10 passed Ali Sherghi~ At 2,,20 P.M. arrived at Amara landed 55 Packages & 33 passengers (2 of the 1st Class)~ We shipped 88 Bales of wool from the Press, No body is pressing at Lynch’s, they have have gone to Eassay’s, Press here as they have also lowered the charge
 A hot N.W. Wind is blowing
~ At 4,,30 P.M. left Amara took 5 passengers~ wind died away and became very calm and misty with some clouds on the southern parts
 At 7,,40 anchored above Kalat Saleh~ Light N.W. air & fine,
