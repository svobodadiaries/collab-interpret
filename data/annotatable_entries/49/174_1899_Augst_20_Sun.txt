20 Sund Therm @ 4.73 Noon 91 4 P.M. 96 [A49_335_03:200] 1899 Augst Draft 2,,6 Barge 181,644 Modte N.W. & fine cool morning, at 7 am I came on board
 I had written two letters for Schweiger Vienna & for Rezooki Metchich London, dated 19th, in which I enclosed a letter for Alexander dated 18th 
 but Metchich letter I could not send as I wanted to enclose for him 2 Shillings Postal order for Stamps he is putting on Alexanders letters to America
 & the Post office is closed, so I gave it to Rezooki Sayegh to send it by next week’s mail
 At 8 ½ AM the SS. Brookside left for London
 ~ It blew fresh with some fine dust but the day was cool throughout 
 We shipped some cargo from the Barges & got the Barge alongside to be towed up
 ~ Very Light N.W. air but not warm, Yousif Marine, Rufail, Rezooki & the Angoorlies & many others came onboard to see Jeboory Asfar going up with us, ~ We took them up in tow to Rufail’s house
