8’ Wedn Light N.W. & fine weather at 8 am called on Sister Medula & at 9 came on board
 the Mossul went inside to discharge
 We are shipping some cargo, at 11 went to breakfast & returned at 2 ½ P.M.
 We shipped galls & wool
 At 4 ½ I went home & at 6 Eliza & I went over to Sister Medula & spent the evening Johny & Artin also came there We left at 8 & came home,
