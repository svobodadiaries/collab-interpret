11 Sund Therm@4.72 [A49_194_14:133] At 0,,50 am left Coot Very nice & cool weather, Took 29 Passengers from Coot
 At 8,,30 passed Memlah, weather very calm
 At 3,,20 P.M. passed the S.S. Khalifah going down at Sherhan (below Humenyeh) Michail Yaghchi was on board going to sell his liquorice which he is collecting at Hilla from Menahem Daniels property
 At 5 P.M. passed Azizieh Light N.W. breeze & fine, The river has fallen a good deal & begun to get shoal in many places, sand bank are appearing ~ At 8,,45 P.M. passed Baghdadieh Fort ~ Fine cool night ~
