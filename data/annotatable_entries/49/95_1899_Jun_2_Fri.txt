2 Frid Light & variable breeze Warm Weather~ At 7 Am I came on board, & then went over to the Residency to see the auction sale
 I bought only a Bookshelve for 12 12 Rps & 1 anna
 Came home It is very warm & sultry, light S. Erly air with some clouds
~ At 4 P.M. I called on Eliza & Adoola my cousins
 Johny & Artin called in the evening
 Very calm & warm weather
 The SS. Baghdad & Barge left this morning at 4 Am
