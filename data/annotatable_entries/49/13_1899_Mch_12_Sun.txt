12 Sund Th@6.42 1899 Mch Modte
 N.W. & fine cold weather ~ At 5,30 Am we left Maghessil, at 5,,40 passed Ali Gherbi
 At 1 P.M. arrived at Amara landed 22 Passengs (Abdulrezack Khdery in 1st Class Cabin) and 52 Packages
 ~Took 5 Tons of Coal~ At 2,,20 P.M. left Amara took 8 Passengers ~ At 5,,30 passed Kalat Saleh At 6.10 anchored at the Canal of Abdulla ibn Ali for the night
