19’ Sund 1899 Mch Holy day of St. Joseph S. Ely wind & cloudy at 8 am I went to church a high mass with music & organ
 we visited the priests, Pere Pièrre (who is now the prefect superior in place of the late Père Marie Joseph) I then called on Asfar, Antone Marine, Habib Chiha, Yaghchi, Sister Medula & came home, many people called on us to wish me the holy day of my patron St Joseph
 ~ I called on Yousif Tessy, Pehlewan & at 3 P.M. with Antone Marine on Yousif Korkis & Sister Eliza & came home at 5 ~ It rained in the daytime too, & weather keeps cloudy
