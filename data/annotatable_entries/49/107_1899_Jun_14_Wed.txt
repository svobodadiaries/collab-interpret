14' Wedn 1899 [A49_198_08:136] Light N.W. air & cool morning at 7 AM I called on Sister Eliza & then on Menusha & came home at 11 ~ At 4 P.M. Eliza & I called on Antone Marine, and in the evening called on Sister Medula till 9 P.M. I heard that Ibrahim Gejou arrived this afternoon from Paris coming via Beyrout, Damascus & Deir, he had left Paris about 45 days ago, the Armenian son of Thadeus Awannes who had gone to Paris to study the fisique also came with him as a doctor now Light N.W. & fine cool weather
